If Joyfull Were a Person, Who Would It Be?

A 34-year-old woman with two young children. She's college educated, confident, creative, and adventurous. She's busy with daily life and her job, but cares about the quality and ingredients of the products she buys. She loves to wear clothes that are comfortable and chic, from youthful brands like Free People, Madewell, and Anthropologie to more eco-conscious brands like Toms, Birkenstock, and Patagonia, to more upscale linen brands like Seaside Tones. She lives in one of Jacksonville's historic neighborhoods or at the beach with her family, loves traveling, yoga, the Beatles, minimalist bloggers and her dog.

Who does the Joyfull website seek to serve?

Joyfull seeks to serve eco-friendly, ingredient conscious, busy individuals and families in the local Jacksonville area who are looking for a convenient and easy way to purchase natural household products and reduce plastic waste.

Who does this website not seek to serve?

Someone who wants products on demand (in store), does not care about being eco-friendly, or cares solely about price point. Someone who does not generally buy from small local businesses.
